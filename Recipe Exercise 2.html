Sweet Cheesecake

You will need:

  • Sugar
  • Philadelphia Cheese
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Eggs
  • Graham Crackers base

How to make it:

  1. Mix cheese and sugar
  2. Mix eggs and vanilla extract
  3. Add it all up
  4. Put Graham crackers base into the pan
  5. Heat your oven up to 250 degrees
  6. Slowly put the mix of cheese and other ingredients onto the the pan with graham cookies
  7. Put it into the oven and wait 40 mins

Enjoy!
